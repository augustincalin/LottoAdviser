This is a simple Lotto Adviser which will give you the most 10 probable numbers for Lotto.de

How it works?

A number is most probable to appear if:

  1. It's missing for a long time (NotSeen).
  2. It was the least extracted (Occurencies).

The data is taken from here and from here.

The (naive) formula used is:

Probability = (Factor1 * NotSeen) / (Factor2 * Occurencies)

...where:

  • Factor1 is a pondering parameter. Initially this is equal with 1.
  • NotSeen is the number of extractions since the ball is missing.
  • Factor2 is a pondering parameter. Initially this is equal with 1.
  • Occurencies is the total number of extractions of that ball since the beginning.
